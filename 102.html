Travel Tips

  • Always keep digital and physical copies of important documents.
  • Pack versatile clothing that can be layered for different weather.
  • Use packing cubes to organize your luggage efficiently.
  • Inform your bank about your travel dates to avoid card blocks.
  • Download offline maps and travel apps before your trip.
  • Stay hydrated and get enough rest during your travels.
  • Learn a few basic phrases in the local language.
  • Keep emergency contact numbers handy at all times.
